Take a Break - Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-19

Take a Break does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. All settings are stored locally in your browser and never leave your device.

Data We Collect

None.

Data We Store

Only local settings required for the extension to function, such as:

  • Watch duration, countdown duration, snooze duration
  • Bedtime hour and whether bedtime reminders are enabled
  • Enabled websites and any custom domains
  • Temporary in-session timers for open tabs

This data is stored using Chrome's local storage on your device.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties.

Remote Code

The extension does not load or execute any remote code. All scripts are bundled within the extension package.

Contact

If you have questions, contact: your-email@example.com

This policy may be updated if the extension adds new features that affect privacy.
